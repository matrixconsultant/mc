About Us

Engineering Excellence
Since 1992

Matrix Consultants is a Karachi-based MEP engineering consultancy with over three decades of uninterrupted practice across Pakistan and internationally. The firm offers full end-to-end engineering services — from feasibility studies and design through to construction management, commissioning, and maintenance procedures.

We believe in simplicity, indigenous materials, value engineering, and the personal involvement of senior personnel at every project stage. Our portfolio spans hospitals, government buildings, commercial towers, luxury residences, industrial plants, educational institutions, and international projects.
